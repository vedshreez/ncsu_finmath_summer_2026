\begin{itemize}

  \item The fill simulator; state tracking of inventory and Greeks
  \item Pitfalls: look-ahead bias, unrealistic fills, expiry handling
  \item Sharpe/Calmar, drawdown, block-bootstrap confidence intervals
\end{itemize}\end{frame}
\begin{frame}{Lecture 2 (Guest: Fenni Kang, Coincall): Taker Strategies}
\begin{itemize}
  \item When and why takers cross the spread
  \item Signals behind aggressive flow: momentum, liquidations, news
  \item Execution styles: sweep, TWAP/VWAP, iceberg-taking
  \item What taker behaviour means for the maker's order-flow toxicity
\end{itemize}\end{frame}
\begin{frame}{Milestone 3 Deliverable}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Full multi-Greek backtest on 3 months of BTC \& ETH options

  \item Greek exposures bounded; drawdown forensic + taker analysis
  \item Reproducible from config; report \textless{}= 20 pages
\end{itemize}\end{frame}
