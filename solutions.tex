\subsection{Problem Set 2 --- Foundations of Market
Microstructure}\label{problem-set-2-foundations-of-market-microstructure}

\textbf{2.1 Order-book reconstruction (10 pts).} Maintain two price→size
maps. On \texttt{new}, add size; on \texttt{cancel}, subtract and
\textbf{delete the level if it reaches zero}; on \texttt{modify},
replace; on \texttt{trade}, decrement the resting side that was hit.

\emph{Expected:} a clean mid/spread CSV. \emph{Common errors:} not
deleting empty levels (−2); decrementing the wrong side on a trade (−3);

\textbf{2.2 Roll estimator (8 pts).}
\texttt{spread\ =\ 2·sqrt(max(0,\ −cov(Δp\_t,\ Δp\_\{t−1\})))}. It is
typically \textbf{smaller} than the quoted spread because it strips
adverse-selection/impact. \emph{Errors:} sign convention on the
covariance (−3); noting the discrepancy without engaging (−3).

\textbf{2.3 Glosten--Milgrom quote-setter (10 pts).} With informed
fraction \texttt{mu} and value prior, bid/ask are posterior expectations
given the trade direction. Bayesian update of the value distribution
after a buy/sell; the spread is endogenous from the indifference
condition. \emph{Errors:} skipping the Bayes update (−3); bid/ask
swapped (−2); not acknowledging endogeneity (−2).

\textbf{2.4 GM simulation (8 pts).} Average many runs; belief converges
to true value. \emph{Errors:} one realization, not averaged (−2);
non-rational prior (−2).

\textbf{2.5 Toxicity (9 pts).} Realized spread uses a future mid; price
impact is the signed mid move. \emph{Errors:} wrong trade-direction sign
(−3); discussion ignoring the actual numbers (−3).

impact. \emph{Errors:} listing effects without quantifying (−3); no
consequence for the strategy (−3).

\subsection{Problem Set 4 --- Stochastic Optimal
Control}\label{problem-set-4-stochastic-optimal-control}

\textbf{4.1 Merton CRRA (12 pts).} Portfolio share
\texttt{pi*\ =\ (mu-r)/(gammasigma\^{}2)} constant in wealth;
consumption proportional to wealth. \emph{Errors:} wrong ansatz (−4);
missing transversality (−3).

\textbf{4.2 Merton CARA (10 pts).} Dollar amount constant in wealth.
Emit side-by-side CSV. \emph{Errors:} failing to articulate the
structural difference (−2).

\textbf{4.3 Almgren--Chriss (12 pts).} Optimal trajectory solves a
linear ODE; hyperbolic-sinh shape; matches the discrete result.
\emph{Errors:} skipping the variational step (−4); wrong boundary
conditions (−3).

\textbf{4.4 HJB-residual verification (10 pts).} Residual
\textasciitilde1e-10 for the true value function; diverges under
perturbation. Use \texttt{torch.autograd.grad} for the partials.
\emph{Errors:} a ``counterexample'' that does not actually break the
theorem (−3).

\textbf{4.5 Obstacle problem (6 pts).} Projected SOR; solution touches
the obstacle in the contact set, matching classical theory elsewhere.
\emph{Errors:} missing the obstacle/contact set entirely (−3).
